Lies, Damn Lies, And Browser Market Share Statistics

A couple of months ago there was a flurry of tweets about browser market share. I think this article, from which I shameless ripped off the “Lies…” quote, in InfoWorld kicked it off. That said, browser market share statistics are similar to the cosmic background radiation, constantly hitting us from all sides but never with any significant force.

A search for browse market share on Google for the past month produces 45M hits. Limiting the search to the past week drops that number to a mere 5M.1
